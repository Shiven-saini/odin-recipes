Uttapam Recipe

Description

Uttapam is a savory South Indian pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils.

Uttapam is a popular breakfast dish, but it can also be enjoyed as a snack or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped green chilies
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Oil for greasing

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the semolina (sooji) in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine the semolina, rice flour, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, coriander leaves, salt, turmeric powder, and red chili powder in a bowl.
  3. Add enough water to make a thick batter.
  4. Heat a greased griddle or tawa. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the griddle and spread it into a circle.
  5. Flip the uttapam and cook the other side for another minute or two.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
